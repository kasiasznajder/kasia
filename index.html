Kasia Sznajder

I’m a creative producer working with clients in food, art, design, fashion, and music to create unique experiences, direct creative collaborations and to define and develop brand strategy.

Past projects I’ve worked on include: creating a digital strategy for 99 Scott, organising VIP dinners for CHART Art Fair, throwing GANNI fashion after-parties, running a pop-up cafe for HAY during Salone del Mobile, producing and managing the 25th anniversary party for Nicolai Wallner Gallery, coordinating socials for Atelier September and Apollo Bar, producing a ceramic retreat for KANA in France. I was an assistant to Kim Hastraiter and Frederik Bille Brahe.
