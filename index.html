Kasia Sznajder

I’m a creative producer working with clients in food, art, design, fashion, and music to create experiential programs, to direct creative collborations and to define and develop brand strategy.

Past projects I’ve worked on include: producing a ceramic retreat for KANA London in France, organizing a dinner for CHART Art Fair, throwing GANNI fashion after-parties, running a pop-up cafe for HAY during Salone del Mobile.

In 2017, I began the collaborative life drawing event series Dionysos Croquis with William Ek-Uvelius. Events have taken place in Copenhagen, Malmö and Berlin, with future events planed in Athens, Barcelona, and New York.

You can get in touch with me via email at katsznajder@gmail.com, or find me on Instagram and Linkedin.
